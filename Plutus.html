Destination 2022

Plutus

Would it be hard to believe that this potato shaped celestial mini-body smaller than the island of Hawaii contains enough metals to make it more valuable than the entire global economy of Earth? Because it does.
Putus has an orbital period of 20 earth years and it resides within an asteroid belt at a distance ranging from 2.5 to 3.3 AU from its system’s Star. Its distance from your team’s current settlement is 370 million km. 
The asteroid itself has no atmosphere due to its almost negligible gravity. It has a diameter of 147km and mass less than 1% of Earth’s Moon.

The incredible value ( $700 quintillion in heavy metals) of the asteroid as the core is thought to be made up of exposed metallic iron, nickel, and gold, based on observations from its reflective properties. The Artificial settlement in which you and your team as currently situated is assumed to be a full fledged orbiting space settlement, with a population of about 5,000. The settlement orbits around the lower orbit of your native planet.In addition, all resources required for interplanetary travel can be assumed to be at your team's disposal.Your company’s primary goal is to mine this asteroid, an activity that has never been achieved in human space exploration history yet. 

Questions:

1.Given that the exact composition of the recently discovered asteroid has not been determined yet, state the steps your company would take to determine the exact metals and minerals composing the asteroid, from ground observations and/or sending probes/rovers. 

2.State the steps your company would undertake to perform mining operations on Plutus, also explain how you would transport the material and how refining processes will take place at the space settlement.
