Destination 2022

Strio

Type: Dry,desert type planet.

It is a Beige and Hazel Colored, desolate world orbiting a pair of binary stars having a surface temperature of 50-60 Celsius during the day. Due to a scarcity of water and moisture content in the planet’s water cycle the life forms found are mostly microbial in nature. Strio orbits a binary star system giving it a distinct two suns in its horizon. Making one of the only tatooine like planets that could potentially habitable., The larger star in the solar system called Rethos-A has a Mass of  2.063±0.023M☉ whereas the smaller star called Rethos-B has a Mass of 1.018 ± 0.011M☉. The surface of Strio is continent-like with mountain ranges and depressions, features which hint towards the planet once having ocean-like surface water.

It has an atmosphere similar to earth with mainly 17.2%-Oxygen, 62.9%-Nitrogen & 19.3%-CO2, 0.3% other. The pressure exerted by the atmosphere is nearly thrice that of Earth leading to a sense of suffocation.
The diameter of Strio is smaller than Earth by roughly 2000 kms (10,236 kms) and has a mass of 5.87 x 10^24 kg.
Though the planet shares a lot of physical similarity with earth. The atmosphere is free of clouds due to the lack of moisture content. The traces of sulphur dioxide (SO2) in the surface makes long term human settlement a challenge.

Questions:

1. Walkthrough the process necessary to make this planet hospitable to humans.

2. Do you, as a Astrophysicist of a research organisation, think the presence of a Binary Star system affects the prospect of mankind’s permanent settlement on this planet?
