Destination 2022

Eos b-315, AKA, Dirt.

Type: Rocky and Mountainous with small/shallow water bodies present on the surface.
This planet potentially lives up to the name of Earth’s younger brother, “Dirt”, …..twice the size….Having a surface temperature of 30-55 celsius, water and water-ice is present in multiple places over the surface and the deeper subsurfaces of the planet as well as, the Poles. Gravity 1800 cm/sq.sec due to its mass being 3.2 times that of Earth’s and radius 1.5 times Earth’s.In further similarity, its orbital distance is 1.064 AU, orbital period- 385 earth days and rotational period 4 earth daysThe atmosphere, however, contains a large percentage of greenhouse gases causing a devastating greenhouse effect. (CO2 34.7%, CH4 28.3%, O2 13.6%, NO 12.4%, CFC-12 6.2%, 4.2 Ar, 0.5% other)

The terrain consists of barren dry land made of high mountains, hills and large craters (upto 600km in diameter). What is most unaccany about this planet’s atmosphere and surface is the extreme amounts of radiation present within it due to massive fragments (500-2000 metres in height and average weight of 10-50kg) scattered across almost half the entirety of the surface of the Planet.Possibility of previous nuclear attack due high radiation or from celestial destruction
