Destination 2022

Saqiurs-34

Planets type – Waterworld, covered in a seemingly endless, shallow ocean which.
Lifeforms – Has aquatic lifeforms. Background:The planet's gravity is described to be "punishing" at 130% of Earth's, forcing human astronauts to move slowly and with some difficulty while on its surface. Time on the surface on Saquirus-34 passes very slowly relative to the time experienced by that experienced on Earth. There is no sign of dry land on Saqiurs-34, which may not exist because of the sheer volume of water on the planet. The ocean was described to smell like distilled water poured on hot rocks - a mineral scent, but not like salt water. The waves and shallow, sterile oceans would not support an environment capable of harbouring advanced life (much less a civilization). It suit’s microbes and other simple lifeforms, due to the presence of necessary hydrocarbons, water, and starlight.
